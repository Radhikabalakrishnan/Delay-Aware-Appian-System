DELAY DECISION LOGIC

Parameters:
- Active cases
- Pending cases
- Average processing time

Rules:
If active_cases < 50:
    No delay (immediate processing)

If active_cases between 50 and 100:
    Delay processing by 10 minutes

If active_cases > 100:
    Delay processing by 60 minutes

Objective:
Prevent overload and SLA violations.
